## Power BI Dashboard Overview

The interactive dashboard is divided into three main pages, each answering a specific business question.

### 1. Executive Overview
**Purpose:** High-level performance snapshot

**Key insights:**
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Month-ON-Month growth
- Best-performing coffee by revenue and orders

### 2. Product & Revenue Analysis
**Purpose:** Understand which products drive business value

**Key insights:**
- Revenue vs order volume by coffee type
- Coffee-wise revenue contribution
- Best selling coffee

### 3. Time Intelligence Analysis
**Purpose:** Identify sales patterns over time

**Key insights:**
- Monthly orders trend
- Monthly revenue trend
- Best revenue month

##  Business Insights Derived
- A small number of coffee products contribute to a large share of revenue.
- Peak revenue and order volume occur during specific months, indicating seasonal demand.
- Certain coffees are ordered frequently but generate lower revenue, while others are high-value products.
